# Contoso Construction Group HSE Policy 2025

## Zero Harm Commitment
Contoso Construction Group is committed to Zero Harm across all operations.
Target: Zero fatalities, Lost Time Injury Frequency Rate (LTIFR) < 1.0

## Incident Reporting
- All incidents must be reported within 24 hours
- Near-misses require reporting within 48 hours
- Critical incidents trigger immediate stop-work authority

## PPE Requirements
All personnel on company sites must wear:
- Hard hat (AS/NZS 1801)
- Safety glasses (AS/NZS 1337)
- High-visibility vest (AS/NZS 4602)
- Steel-capped boots (AS/NZS 2210)
- Hearing protection in zones exceeding 85dB

## Risk Management
All work activities require a Job Safety Analysis (JSA) before commencement.
High-risk activities require a Safe Work Method Statement (SWMS):
- Working at heights (>2m)
- Excavation and trenching
- Confined space entry
- Hot works
- Crane operations
- Electrical work

## Key Metrics (FY2024)
- LTIFR: 0.82 (target: <1.0) ✓
- Total Recordable Injury Frequency Rate (TRIFR): 3.1
- Near-miss reporting rate: 12.4 per million hours worked
- Safety observations submitted: 45,200
- Stop-work authorities exercised: 312

## Environmental Management
- All projects must have an Environmental Management Plan (EMP)
- Waste recycling target: >75% by weight
- Carbon emissions reporting: Monthly to sustainability team
- Water management: Zero uncontrolled discharge to waterways
